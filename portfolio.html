stem

  1. custom smart home technology

    Smart tech and home automation has remained very costly. Because of these prohibitory price tags, I enjoy extending the functionality of my living space without buying anything extra; I actively contribute to the home automation community and create personal plugins for furnature like my ceiling fan.

  2. 3d printing

    3d-printing has always been one of my favorite hobbies as it allows for extremely quick part prototyping and fabrication. By working on small projects for tools, replacement parts, custom lego pieces, and other various projects, I have become proficient in 3d-modeling with openscad and used my printing abilities for several commercial clients. Please contact me for 3d-print pricing on my contact page.

  3. minecraft bedrock content creation

    I created and ran Grind Studios—a development group focused on creating engaging gameplay for players and tools for other creators to make projects never before possible. All of the free content created by Grind Studios has ammassed over 220,000 downloads; you can find the content I created independently on my MCPEDL page. You can find my favorite map that I created with other studios on Minecraft's marketplace.

  4. cht

    The CHT was a weekly Minecraft tournament created by Grind Studios that included custom, one of a kind games that took after vanilla parkour courses, spleef, survival games, sumo, and battle drop (a combination of survival games and spleef). Each game included scalable game mechanics and balancing as well as in-depth data processing for players and team coaches. This tournament was played over the course of eight weeks with players from around the world.

  5. coyote

    Coyote is a discord bot built to create embedded messages, polls, live timers, and giveaways with simple commands. Before it was discontinued (as it was made redundant by Discord's own built-in features), the app had hundreds of active users and used the newest features availible in Discord's API.
