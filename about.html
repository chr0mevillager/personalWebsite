About

Hello there! My name, as the website name implies, is Chase. I am a computer science major at the Colorado School of Mines and a follower of Christ.

Skills & Interests

I enjoy programming (mostly IOS apps and discord bots) and working on designs (primarily logos) with friends. I also have several years of experience in robotics including vex, ev3, and arduino/raspi as well as photography, cinematography, and stage lighting. You can find my favorite projects in my portfolio. In all of my projects, I always maintain a high level of quality by focusing on mathematical, exacting precision and always keep extreme attention to detail. For this reason, I my work often acts as a synthesis of form and function in technical areas and the arts.

I also do a lot of video gaming, listen to music, and watch a lot of movies/TV shows. My all-time favorite games include Limbo and Beat Saber. Juelz and Stonebank are my top musical artists. My favorite movie is Inception and favorite show is Severence.
